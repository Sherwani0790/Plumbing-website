Water Treatment

We want to ensure that water supplies are safe for the end user. Certain areas of concern have been addressed by the local authority and government agencies. Mr. Mike’s Plumbing plays an important role in ensuring plumbing supplies, pipework and water storage tanks are clean and free of harmful bacteria in accordance with Acop L8.
On new plumbing and pipework installations it is generally considered good practice to have the pipework thoroughly disinfected and chlorinated.
This reduces the risk of infection being passed to the end user from substances used in the installation and from any bacteria that is present in the pipework or water. With the growing concern and more and more outbreaks of Legionaires disease as a result of poor water supplies, Mr. Mike’s Plumbing offers a legionella testing service with recommendations of preventative measures.

Our Work

Plumbing involves the installation, repair, and maintenance of systems that provide water supply, drainage, heating, and sanitation in various types of buildings. Plumbers are responsible for installing piping systems, fixtures, and appliances, ensuring the safe and efficient distribution of water and disposal of wastewater. They perform tasks such as detecting and repairing leaks, clearing blockages, and conducting routine inspections and maintenance. Plumbers must interpret blueprints, design system layouts, and select appropriate materials, all while adhering to local building codes and safety regulations. Effective communication with clients, problem-solving skills, and technical proficiency are essential for diagnosing issues and providing solutions. The work environment ranges from residential homes to commercial and industrial sites, requiring physical stamina and the ability to work in various conditions. Training typically involves apprenticeships or technical education, with opportunities for certification and career advancement in specialized areas or supervisory roles..
